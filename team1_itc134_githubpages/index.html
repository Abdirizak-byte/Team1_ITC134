What is Volunteer Computing? Volunteer computing is an arrangement in which volunteers provide computing resources to projects, which use the resources (processing power, storage, and Internet connection) of volunteers to do distributed computing and/or storage. Volunteers are typically anonymous members of the general public, organizations can also act as volunteers and contribute their computing resources; projects are typically academic (university-based) and do scientific research.
